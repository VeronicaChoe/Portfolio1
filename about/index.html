About

Hi! I'm Veronica Choe, a designer based in NY and NJ, with a passion for design, technology, and motion. I recently finished my BFA in Communication Design with a minor in Comics and Graphic Narrative at Parsons School of Design.

This collection is an evolving display of my best work. My work spans typography, web, motion, book design, branding and more - always with a focus on transforming ideas into visually captivating experiences. When I'm not designing, I love playing board games, going to museums, and bullet journaling!
